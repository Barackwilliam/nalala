Discover the Hidden Oasis

The Chemka Hot Springs, also known as Kikuletwa Hot Springs, is a hidden oasis between Arusha and Moshi. Surrounded by lush trees and crystal-clear turquoise water, it's the perfect place to relax, swim, and enjoy nature.

Despite the name "hot springs," the water is warm, fresh, and beautifully refreshing - offering the perfect escape from the African heat.
